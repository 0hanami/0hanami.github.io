Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ieKarte stores all user-entered home management data (maintenance schedules, appliance information, repair records, etc.) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Advertising

The free version of ieKarte displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers (IDFA) for ad delivery. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. Data Storage

All user data is stored on your device. If you use the family sharing feature, data is shared through Apple iCloud.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

6. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, please contact us through the support link in the App Store.
