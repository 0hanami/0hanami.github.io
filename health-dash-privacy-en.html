Health Dash Privacy Policy

Introduction

Health Dash (hereinafter "the App") is an iOS application provided by the developer 0hanami. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1-1. HealthKit Data

The App reads and writes the following health data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Activity data such as steps, calories burned, and exercise duration
  • Body measurement data such as weight and body fat percentage
  • Sleep data

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. Access to HealthKit data requires your explicit permission.

1-2. In-App Data

App settings and dashboard configurations are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. No data is sent to external servers.

1-3. Account Information

The App does not require account registration. We do not collect personal information such as usernames or email addresses.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) - only with user consent
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • Information about ad impressions and clicks

For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

The App complies with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework and requests user permission before performing any tracking. If you do not allow tracking, you can still use all features of the App. Non-personalized ads will be displayed instead of personalized ads.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit. Payment processing is managed by Apple. The developer does not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App will remove all in-app data. Data written to HealthKit can be managed separately through the Health app.

6. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share user data with third parties, except for the information necessary for ad delivery as described in Section 2.

7. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

Users residing in the European Economic Area (EEA) have data protection rights under the GDPR. Since the App does not store personal data on external servers, data deletion is completed by deleting the App. For information about data handling related to advertising, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated without prior notice. When changes are made, this page will be updated along with the revision date.
